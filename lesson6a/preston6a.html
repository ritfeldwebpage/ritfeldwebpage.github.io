Normally we were busy with the preparations, because next Sunday it is already the first Sunday of July. This year unfortunately no Picnic Festival, but we look back with a smile

A picnic is a meal in the open air, somewhere other than on a restaurant terrace or at the house. You can have a picnic in the forest, in a park or on the beach. It can be placed on the floor and on furniture brought along such as folding chairs and possibly a folding table. When it is on the floor, a rug, sheet, or towel is sometimes spread.

Picnic comes from the French Pique nique, and has been used since the 17th century. [1] Pique means 'choose something' while nique means 'something small'. It therefore refers to the different types of food that are displayed on the rug and from which the participants can choose.
