controller
www.controller.com

A general aviation trade publication serving the needs of buyers and sellers of single- and twin-engine piston aircraft since its debut in 1980, Controller features used aircraft and detailed aircraft for-sale listings. Controller offers full-color photos in a clean and easy-to-read layout to provide a comprehensive display of aircraft and aviation products and services available throughout North America. Published weekly, Controller is distributed to subscribers, aircraft owners, and FBOs and provides the most current listings and information possible to those in the market.
